# User Story: Visit Trends

**As** a business stakeholder
**I want** to calculate the number of visits per day, week, and month
**So that** I can track growth and engagement over time.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Reports exist for daily, weekly, and monthly visit counts.
- Trends are visualized or summarized in reports.
- Data is based on deduplicated silver data and partitioned by event date.
